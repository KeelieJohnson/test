About us

Pat's Cafe

"The Home of the SlugBurger"

Born in Savannah, Georgia, Ms. Knight began her trip to founding Pat's Cafe after her graduation from high school. After spending several years of working in the cafeterias of McNairy Counties schools, a friend of hers who recently bought a cafe asked if she could manage it. In January of 1988, she bought the business and renamed it Pat’s Cafe. Today, the shop is ran by her daughter-in-law Temara Knight who carries on the business and continues to make the shop the ‘Home of the Slugburger”. You can find the shop at 110 E. Court Ave, Selmer, Tn 38375.
